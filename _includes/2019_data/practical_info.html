Course fee

100 EUR per participant (to be paid within 30 days after being accepted to the course)

The fee covers: Course material, lectures and workshops, lunch and coffee breaks, course dinner on Thursday night. Accommodation, travel and remaining dinners should be covered by participants (see information below)

Course credits

Doctoral students from Eurotech institutions can obtain 2 ECTS for completing this Summer School (if relevant). The course is part of the EPFL doctoral school program (course description).

Non-Eurotech PhD candidates who wish to register for ECTS credits should check with their own institution for the possibility and procedure to receive the credits.

Requirements and workload for obtaining credits are as follow:

  • Participation to the full program

  • Pre-course homework (ca 8 hours to be completed 1 week prior to the course)

  • Post-course homework (ca 8 hours to be completed 2 weeks after the course)

Accommodation

Participants should book their own accommodation and travel as soon as they receive confirmation of their participation from the organising team. We can recommend the following options:

  • Starling Hotel - on campus, 10 minutes walk from EPFL metro station

  • Swisstech Hotel - on campus, right at the EPFL metro station

  • Mad House - in Lausanne city center, very central, in style, right at a metro station, 15 minutes to EPFL

  • Ibis Hotel - in Lausanne city center, relatively central, 5 minutes from a metro station, 20 minutes to EPFL

If you need any more information or recommendations, you can contact us by email.

Travel

We believe it is reasonable for you to arrive the night before (Sunday 1st September) and leave on the afternoon of the last day (Friday 6th September).

Participants are responsible for the reimbursement of your travel costs.

By plane

The closest airport to EPFL is Geneva International (GVA). As an alternative, you can fly to Zürich airport (ZRH). However, please note that, although there is a direct train link from Zürich airport to Lausanne, the travel time is significantly longer.

From Geneva airport (GVA) to Lausanne: direct train connection, 45-60 minutes

From Zürich airport (ZRH) to Lausanne: direct train connection, 2 hours 30 minutes

By train

International train connections make it easy to travel to Lausanne e.g. from France, Germany and Italy. Swiss trains can be scheduled and booked with the SBB. Please consider this option to reduce your carbon footprint.

Local public transport

Most hotel deals will come with a free public transportation card for the duration of your stay. Do ask the reception upon check in. You can also buy regular tickets from any stop.

From Lausanne main train station, you can reach EPFL in about 25 minutes using the Metro (Line M2) to station “Lausanne Flon” and then line M1 from “Lausanne Flon” towards “Renens”, stop “Ecublens EPFL”.
